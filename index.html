Research & Projects

Hausa Sentiment Classification using Deep Learning

Role: Lead Research Assistant | Tools: BiLSTM, FastText | Year: 2024

Objective: Build a Hausa-language sentiment analysis model to evaluate public opinion from social media.
Methodology: Collected and annotated 10K movie reviews; applied text cleaning, tokenization, and BiLSTM with FastText embeddings.
Outcome: Achieved 85% accuracy, outperforming logistic baseline models.
Societal Impact: Supports digital media literacy and helps organizations understand local sentiment trends for development communication.

Low-Resource Neural Machine Translation (Hausa ↔ English)

Role: Model Developer | Tools: Transformer, Masakhane Corpus | Year: 2023

Objective: Improve translation accuracy for underrepresented African languages using transfer learning.
Methodology: Fine-tuned a Transformer-based NMT model on parallel Hausa-English datasets using Masakhane pipeline.
Outcome: BLEU score improvement of +5 points compared to baseline.
Societal Impact: Enhances access to educational and health information for Hausa-speaking communities.

Bias and Fairness in African NLP Models

Role: Research Contributor | Tools: XLM-R, Sentiment Datasets | Year: 2024

Objective: Identify and mitigate gender, regional, and occupational bias in African NLP models.
Methodology: Conducted comparative bias evaluations using token attribution and fairness metrics.
Outcome: Proposed bias mitigation recommendations for low-resource settings.
Societal Impact: Encourages ethical AI practices and equitable language technology deployment.
